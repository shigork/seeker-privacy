Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

SEEKER ("we", "our", or "us") is a peer-to-peer location tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the SEEKER mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location data: Your GPS coordinates, updated in real time while the app is active, so connected friends can see your position.
  • Device identifiers: A pseudonymous user ID generated at registration, stored locally and on our backend.
  • Codename / display name: The name you choose when registering.
  • Bluetooth & Wi-Fi data: Used exclusively for peer-to-peer (P2P) device discovery via Android Nearby Connections. We do not store Bluetooth or Wi-Fi scan results.
  • Crash & diagnostic data: Collected automatically via Firebase Crashlytics to help us identify and fix bugs.
  • Analytics events: Aggregated, anonymised usage events collected via Firebase Analytics (e.g. screens viewed, features used).
  • Purchase information: Subscription status managed by RevenueCat. We do not store payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display your real-time location to friends you have explicitly connected with inside the app.
  • Enable peer-to-peer encrypted messaging and location sharing over Bluetooth/Wi-Fi.
  • Process and manage in-app subscriptions.
  • Detect and fix crashes and performance issues.
  • Improve the app based on aggregated usage patterns.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following service providers, each bound by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase – backend database and authentication.
  • Firebase (Google) – crash reporting and analytics.
  • RevenueCat – subscription and purchase management.

Your location is shared peer-to-peer only with the specific friends you have added and connected with inside SEEKER. We do not share your location with any third parties.

4. Data Retention

Your location data is updated in real time and is not archived. Account data (codename, user ID) is retained until you delete your account. You can delete your account from the Settings screen inside the app.

5. Children's Privacy

SEEKER is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Permissions

  • Location (foreground & background): Required to share your GPS position with friends.
  • Bluetooth & Nearby Devices: Required for peer-to-peer connection discovery.
  • Camera: Used only for the optional AR Live View compass feature.
  • Notifications: Used to alert you when a friend comes online or shares their location.

7. Security

All communication between devices is encrypted. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your data in transit and at rest.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, delete your account from within the app or contact us at the address below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
